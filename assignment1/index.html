Why Alt School?

I joined AltSchoolAfrica to pursue my software engineering career with an unwavering passion that burns deep within me. This decision is not just a career choice; it's a commitment to a lifelong journey of self-discovery, problem-solving, and creative expression through code. There are several compelling reasons why I embarked on this path, and each one ignites my passion even further.

  • Love for Technology: Ever since I was a child, I've been enamored with technology. From my first encounter with a computer to exploring the limitless possibilities of the internet, I've always been fascinated by how technology can shape and improve our lives.
  • Problem Solver at Heart: I have always been a natural problem solver. There is an indescribable joy that comes from breaking down complex issues into manageable components and finding elegant solutions. Software engineering is the ultimate platform for this innate skill.
  • Creativityand Innovation: Software engineering offers an exciting canvas for creative expression. Through code, I can bring my ideas to life, whether it's a groundbreaking mobile app, a powerful web platform, or a game-changing software solution. The boundless potential for innovation in this field is exhilarating.
  • Changing the World: In today's digital age, software engineers are at the forefront of shaping the future. I believe that technology has the power to transform industries, solve global challenges, and make the world a better place. Being a software engineer means being a part of this transformative process.
  • Continuous Learning: The tech industry is dynamic, constantly evolving. This continuous learning journey is both thrilling and challenging. I am passionate about the idea of staying on the cutting edge, adapting to new technologies, and expanding my knowledge base.
  • Community and Collaboration: AltSchoolAfrica emphasizes collaboration, mentorship, and community. The prospect of learning alongside like-minded individuals and being part of a supportive network of aspiring and established software engineers fuels my passion. This sense of community and shared goals is invaluable.
  • Financial Independence: Let's not forget the practical aspect. A career in software engineering offers the potential for financial independence, stability, and opportunities for personal growth. This provides the freedom to pursue my passions and dreams outside of work.
